Singy for Android is finally released!

10/18/2014

Like many people, I like to sing. I sing karaoke with my friends. I sing in the shower.

However, I still sing off-pitch.

To solve that, I made Singy.

Singy gives you the ability to practice sustaining pitches.

It grades you on how precise you hold the pitch and how long you sustain it.

If you sing off-pitch like me, use Singy!
